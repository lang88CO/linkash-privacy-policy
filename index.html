This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Linkash application.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We only collect the minimum information required to operate the service:

- Personal Information you provide:

  • Full Name: Used to generate your unique referral link.
  • Email Address: Used for account creation, login, and communication.
  • PayPal Email Address: Used solely to process your withdrawal requests. We do not store or share this information for any other purpose.

- Automatically Collected Information:

  • Unique user ID: Assigned by our secure authentication system (Firebase).
  • Transaction Data: Record of your earnings, referrals, and withdrawal history.
  • Basic Device Information: Collected automatically to ensure app stability and performance (no personal identifiers).

We DO NOT collect: Location data, contacts, photos, files, browsing history, or sensitive financial details (credit card/bank numbers are handled directly by Google Play and PayPal).

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

Your data is used only to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Generate your unique referral link.
  • Track successful referrals and update your balance.
  • Process payments and withdrawals automatically.
  • Maintain security and improve app performance.

3. DATA SHARING & THIRD PARTIES

We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

We only use trusted service providers strictly necessary for the app to work:

  • Firebase (Google): For secure authentication, database storage, and analytics.
  • Google Play Billing: To process the one-time download payment.
  • PayPal: To send funds directly to your account upon withdrawal request.

All third parties comply with strict data protection laws.

4. DATA SECURITY

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All information is stored securely and accessed only by authorized systems.

5. DATA RETENTION

We keep your data only as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.

6. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

Linkash is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
